North Estonia Medical Centre

If you need some medical help, please call 617 1300.
Registration desk is available when you call 617 1369.
Address: J. Sütiste tee 19, 13419 Tallinn.

Tallinn Tourist Information Centre

This institution can help you get free city maps, book a tour guide and buy a Talinn Card - the city’s all-in-one sightseeing pass.
It's open every day: 09:00 - 18:00.
Address: Niguliste 2, 10146 Tallinn, Estonia
Phone number: 645 7777

Tallinn Airport

Airport is located just 4 km from the city centre.
It has good public transport connection, offers sufficient access for taxies and provides plenty of options for parking your car.
Address: Tartu maantee 101, 10112 Tallinn, Estonia
Phone number for information: 605 8888
